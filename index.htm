The Representative Group from Myanmar Fishing Enterprise Association has travelled to Vietnam for an excursion tour

  
 

18th June, 2015, Yangon

The general secretary of Myanmar Fishing Enterprise Association,the main association and the concerned associations, and the Excursion Representative Group from Myanmar Fishing Enterprise Association containing 22 members from the Regional Fishing Enterprises’ Main Association have departed to Hochimin city, Vietnam on 16th June for an excursion tour in Vietnam from 16th to 23rd June to discover the Fishing Enterprise of Vietnam, whose Marine Life Enterprise is developing and which has the same soil and water conditions as Myanmar, with the intention of the development of Myanmar Fishing Enterprise.

That group has already discovered the Hochimin city till 18th June and will discover the Kantho University from 19th to 22nd June.

The Representative Group concerning from Myanmar Fishing Enterprise Association which contains 14 members has already gone on a excursion tour to Vietnam for the 1st time from 24th to 31st March 2015.

Reportedly, Myanmar Fishing Enterprise Association is planning to go on excursion tours to Vietnam in 2015 by dividing the Representative Groups concerning Myanmar Fishing Enterprise.

(The Global New Light Myanmar)

  
 



 

  
 

ျမန္မာႏိုင္ငံ ငါးလုပ္ငန္းအဖြဲ႕ခ်ဳပ္မွ ကိုယ္စားလွယ္အဖြဲ႕ ဗီယက္နမ္ႏိုင္ငံသို႕ ေလ့လာေရးခရီးစဥ္သြားေရာက္

  
 

ရန္ကုန္ ဇြန္ ၁၈

ျမန္မာႏိုင္ငံ၏ ငါး/ပုစြန္ ေမြးျမဴေရးလုပ္ငန္းမ်ား ဘက္စံုဖြံ႕ၿဖိဳးတိုးတက္ေစရန္ ရည္ရြယ္ခ်က္ျဖင့္ ျမန္မာႏိုင္ငံႏွင့္ ေရေျမသဘာ၀တူညီၿပီး ေရသတၱ၀ါေမြးၿမဴေရးလုပ္ငန္းမ်ား ဖြံ႕ၿဖိဳးတိုးတက္ေနသည့္ ဗီယက္နမ္ႏိုင္ငံ၏ ငါး/ပုစြန္ေမြးျမဴေရး လုပ္ငန္းမ်ားကို သြားေရာက္ေလ့လာရန္ ျမန္မာႏိုင္ငံ၏ ငါးလုပ္ငန္းအဖြဲ႕ခ်ဳပ္ အေထြအေထြ အတြင္းေရးမွဴး ဦး၀င္းႀကိဳင္ႏွင့္ အဖြဲ႕ခ်ဳပ္ႏွင့္ လုပ္ငန္းဆိုင္ရာအသင္းမ်ား၊ တိုင္းေဒသႀကီး/ျပည္နယ္ ငါးလုပ္ငန္း အဖြဲ႕ခ်ဳပ္မ်ားက အဖြဲ႔၀င္ ၂၂ ဦး ပါ၀င္ သည့္ ျမန္မာ့ငါးလုပ္ငန္းဆိုင္ရာ ေလ့လာေရးကိုယ္စားလွယ္ အဖြဲ႕သည္ ဇြန္ ၁၆ ရက္မွ ၂၃ ရက္အထိ ဗီယက္နမ္ႏိုင္ငံသို႔ ေလ့လာေရး ခရီးသြားေရာက္ရန္ အတြက္ ဇြန္လ ၁၆ ရက္ က ဗီယက္နမ္ႏိုင္ငံ ဟိုခ်ီမင္းၿမိဳ႕သို႔ ထြက္ခြာသြားၾကသည္။

အဆိုပါ အဖဲြ႕သည္ ဇြန္ ၁၈ ရက္ အထိ ဟိုခ်ီမင္းၿမိဳ႕၌ ေလ့လာၿပီး ဇြန္ ၁၉ ရက္မွ ၂၂ ရက္အထိ ကန္သို တကၠသိုလ္သို႔ သြားေရာက္ေလ့လာမည္ျဖစ္သည္။

ျမန္မာႏိုင္ငံငါးလုပ္ငန္းအဖြဲ႕ခ်ဳပ္မွ အဖြဲ႕၀င္ ၁၄ဦးပါ ျမန္မာ့ငါးလုပ္ငန္းဆိုင္ရာ ေလ့လာေရးကိုယ္စားလွယ္ အဖြဲ႕ သည္ ဗီယက္နမ္ႏို္င္ငံသို႕ ၂၀၁၅ ခုႏွစ္၊ မတ္လ ၂၄ ရက္မွ ၃၁ ရက္အထိ ပထမအႀကိမ္ သြားေရာက္ေလ့လာခဲ့ၿပီးျဖစ္သည္။

ဆက္လက္၍ ျမန္မာႏိုင္ငံငါးလုပ္ငန္းအဖြဲ႕ခ်ဳပ္မွ ျမန္မာ့ငါးလုပ္ငန္းဆို္င္ရာ ေလ့လာေရး ကိုယ္စားလွယ္အဖြဲ႕ မ်ားခြဲ၍ ဗီယက္နမ္ႏိုင္ငံသို႔ ၂၀၁၅ ခုႏွစ္ အတြင္း ေလ့လာေရးခရီးစဥ္မ်ား သြားေရာက္ရန္ စီစဥ္ေဆာင္ရြက္လ်က္ ရွိေၾကာင္း သိရသည္။

Government is planning to loosen tie on fuel import procedure

It means that the permission for fuel import would be allowed to organizations and private businesses to apply to the Trade Council. But other organizations must seek an approval from the Trade Council. If approval is granted, the organization has to reveal the amount of fuel and why it is needed in the application. The private sectors that would be given permission are the mining, gems, forestry, fishery and livestock sectors. [Source: Myanmar times]

Legal experts are expecting Trademark act

Legal experts last week said Acts must be put in action to protect trademarks if the country is going to attract the foreign investment. it is also an important task to educate the people about the trademark act. Enacting a trademark act will bring benefits for both businesses and the state. If companies want to register their property, they have to report and work with the Yangon Registration Office of the settlements and Land Records Department. [Source: Myanmar times]
