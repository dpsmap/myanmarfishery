General Information
 
 
Name

Myanmar Fishery and Livestock Fair - 2003
Objectives
 - To development awareness of Myanmar's developing fishery and livestock industries internationally
- To gain market access for the fishery and livestock products.
- To attract foreign investment in fishery and livestock sector.
- To obtain technical know-how of fishery and livestock production and other related manufacturing.
- To produce and share healthy consumer goods, including high quality value added products.
Period
 25 February - 1 March 2003
Time
 09:00 hrs. to 17:00 hrs.
Duration
 5 days
Organizer
 Jointly organized by the Ministry of Commerce and the Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries.
Venue
 Yangon Trade Centre, Upper Pazundaung Road, Yangon, Myanmar.
Exhibition Area
 Three exhibition halls with Indoor area of 89088 sq.ft.
 
(Approx 8280 sq.metre)
 
3m x 3m standard size booths will be provided. Outdoor space will be provided for heavy exhibits. Detailed particulars are to be sent to the Organizing Committee in advance for confirmation.

Exhibition Booths
 Facilities including three partition walls, ceramic tile floor, two fluorescent lamps, a table and two chairs will be provided. Fascia carrying the exhibitor's name is to be decorated by the exhibitor.
Exhibitors

Manufacturers, traders, sale representatives, agents, associations, public organizations in the fishery and livestock business from Myanmar and abroad.
