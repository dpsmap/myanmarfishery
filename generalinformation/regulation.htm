REGULATIONS
APPLICATION FOR PARTICIPATION : In order to be accepted as exhibitors, interested parties shall fill in, sign and submit the application for participation in the prescribed form to the organizer not later than 25 January 2003.
INDEX OF EXHIBITS : To enable the organizer to compile and publish a complete listing of exhibits, all exhibitors are required to complete the detailed list of exhibits in Annexure -C attached to this booklet and shall submit it to the organizer before 25 January 2003.
PAYMENT SCHEDULE FOR PARTICIPANTS : The application shall be accompanied by the payment of50% of the participation fee.
The above payment is not refundable.
Settlement of the balance due for participation shall be made not later than 25 January 2003.
Payment may be made by Bank Draft or Payment Order or Telegraphic Transfer of Travellers' Cheques to the following bank: -

ALLOCATION OF BOOTH : Allocation of booth will be made by the Organizer. The Organizer, however, reserves the right to take into consideration the nature of the exhibits and applicant and space applied for, in allocating the booth.
EXHIBITORS ENTRY PASSES : Those who have paid the participation fee are allowed to enter the booth and entry passes will be issued free of charge to the exhibitors according to the number of persons mentioned in Annexure A.
REFUND OF ADVANCE PAYMENT : In case there is no space available, the organizer will notify the interested party in writing and refund the amount deposited with the application for participation to the beneficiary without additional formalities.
IMPORTATION AND CUSTOM CLEARANCE OF GOODS : Goods brought into the Trade Fair may include exhibit materials as mentioned in Annexure B and installation materials, office materials, decoration materials, and items such as small gifts, souvenirs, literatures, promotional items.
    Guidelines for Dispatch of Goods

The exhibitors shall follow the under mentioned guidelines to dispatch their goods for the smooth and timely clearance and movement to the Trade Fair site: -

  1. 20 containers preferably shall be used.

  2. The exhibits to be dispatched shall be of reasonable quantity or volume for the booths and / or space hired and this quantity / volume must be approved by the organizing committee before they are dispatched. Any excess over that quantity / volume shall be confiscated by the authorities.

  3. Full container load (FCL) rather than less container load (LCL) shall be used by exhibitors individually or collectively.

  4. Local representatives or notified agents of foreign exhibitors shall accept the exhibits at the exhibition\site at the time when they are transported from bonded warehouses to the Trade Fair site.

  5. The exhibit materials shall be cleared, transported and forwarded by N .M. T Ltd.

  6. Goods destined for the Trade Fair must be dispatched on CIF basis. All shipments must be accompanied with the following documents and in accordance with the following guidelines

INSURANCE : Exhibitors are recommended to insure exhibits against all risks for the whole period (round trip) on your own responsibility.
CUSTOMS DUTY :
  1. Drawback Items

    Import license fee, customs duty and other taxes for the goods
    which are brought into the Fair site are not imposed under the
    condition that the goods be re-exported as draw- back cargo .
     
  2. Sale Items

    If the goods are not re-exported for the reasons of sales,
    license fee, customs duty, income tax and commercial tax must
    be paid by the participants, exhibitors.
     
  3. Other Items

    1. The exhibitors can donate the exhibits to Private Companies, Joint Ventures, Embassies and other organizations for charity and any other purposes, by paying
      import license fee, customs duties and other taxes.
    2. Exhibitors shall pay import license fee, customs duties and taxes for the exhibits, which are left with their local agents or branch offices in Myanmar after the Trade Fair.
    3. The import license fee, customs duties and other taxes shall not be waived for exhibition construction and decoration materials which are used and not to be re- exported as draw -back cargo.
OPERATION OF BOOTHS : The exhibitor is required to display the declared products and to man the stand with adequate and competent staff during the opening business hours of the Fair. The exhibitors shall not undertake or cause to undertake any activity which, in the opinion of the organizer, is likely to cause sensitively I annoyance to visitors or other exhibitors.
SALES OF PRODUCTS : Sales of products are permitted, but exhibitors must ensure that there are adequate quantity of products declared to put on display at their stand through out the period of the Fair.
PUBLICITY : Exhibitors may place signboards, posters, notices, printed matters and such, in their stands or pavilions free of charge. Brochures and leaflets can only be distributed within the enclosure of booths or pavilions.
The placing of any publicity material or the installation of illuminated signs outside the pavilion shall be subject to the prior approval of the Organizing Committee and they will be charged according to the rates prescribed.
EXHIBITION OFFICIAL HANDBOOK : One free official handbook is provided for each exhibitor. The official handbook includes complete data of the Fair and certain advertisements. All exhibitors are entitled to a free listing of their products in the Exhibition Official Handbook.
COMMUNICATION FACILITIES : Telephone booths including international direct dialing and facsimile service be available in the premise of the Fair.
CLEANING SERVICES : The Organizing Committee will be responsible for cleaning the aisles and outer areas of the pavilion, It is however the exhibitor's responsibility to maintain cleanliness of their individual booths.
RE-EXPORTATION OF EXHIBITS : All exhibits and the materials used for display must be removed from the venue not later than 41h March 2003. For security reason, exhibitors are required to notify the Security Affairs Committee the removal of the exhibits out of the venue.
Removal of the exhibits during the opening hour and / or before the closing date of the Fair is strictly prohibited. Deadline for re-exportation of exhibit items in transit is two weeks after the end of the Fair. Exhibit which have been imported for the Fair and have not been re-exported
within the time limit will be considered to be unclaimed goods and disposed off by the Organizing Committee.
CENSORSHIP OF AUDIO-VISUAL PRODUCTS : All films, audio-visual tapes, disc, diskettes or any other that are to be displayed or sold at the Fair must have the prior approval of the Myanmar Censorship Board through organizers.
GENERAL SECURITY : The organizer shall undertake to maintain general security surveillance. The general security arrangement does not cover the security of exhibits
of each individual exhibitor in their own booths. Exhibitors are required
to arrange at their own expense the specific security measures in
accordance with type of their exhibits.
ENTRY VISA : The participants can approach Myanmar Embassy / Consulate General Office there and get the entry visa by producing their application for the participation of the Fair.
